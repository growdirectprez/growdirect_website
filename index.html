About GrowDirect

\

GrowDirect is a DAO on Avalanche C-Chain, funding regenerative farming (e.g., shade-grown coffee, hydroponic greens) with the GrowToken (GDT). Our "Cloud Grown" credential ensures transparency, while GrowBarter empowers local trade.

\

Mission: Connect tropical farmers with consumers, sequester carbon, and build resilient communities.
